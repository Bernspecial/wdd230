Membership Level Benefits

How the membership benefits are being shared
Level Benefits
Non-Profit Membership
(No Fee)
  • Discounts on event sponsorships and advertising.
  • Access to exclusive non-profit networking events
  • One free spotlight position on the chamber's home page per year
Bronze Membership
($200/year)
  • All Level 1 benefits
  • Priority access to chamber events and training sessions
  • Opportunities to participate in chamber committees and task forces
Silver Membership
($350/year)
  • All Level 2 benefits
  • Three free spotlight positions on the chamber's home page per year
  • VIP access to chamber events, including exclusive receptions and meetings with elected officials
  • Opportunities to host a chamber event or training session at your non-profit's location
  • Recognition as a Leadership Circle member on the chamber's website and social media channels
Gold Membership
($600/year)
  • All Level 3 benefits
  • Opportunities to co-host a chamber event or training session with the chamber's CEO or Board Chair
  • Recognition as a Visionary member on the chamber's website, social media channels, and print materials
  • Opportunities to co-host a chamber event or training session with the chamber's CEO or Board Chair
  • Access to a dedicated chamber staff liaison for customized support and resources
